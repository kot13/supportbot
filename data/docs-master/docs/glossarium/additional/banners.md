# Banners

Banners are visual elements inside the app that can display images, promotions, or interactive widgets. They are scrollable and can be customized to match the app’s design.

:::warning[Attention!]
This is an additional paid feature. Please contact the sales team to connect it to your project.
:::

Place banners anywhere in your app to highlight campaigns, drive engagement, or show contextual content. You can control how they look, whether they auto-rotate, and how users can navigate between them. Events from banners (such as views or clicks) can also be tracked to measure effectiveness.

Follow these integration guides to add Banners to your app:
- [Android](/sdk-guides/android/banners.md)
- [iOS](/sdk-guides/ios/banners.md)
- [JS](/sdk-guides/js-sdk/banners.md)
